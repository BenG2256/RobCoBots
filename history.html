RobCoBots Team History

Rookie Year: 2017 SteamWorks

The RobCoBots formed in 2017 with a small group of students and mentors and competed in the SteamWorks competitiion season. In 2017, the RobCoBots were Event winners at their first competition the NC District Greensboro event. The RobCoBots were Alliance 7 captains at the NC District Raleigh event, and were awarded the Rookie Inspiration Award. To close out the RobCoBots rookie season, they were Alliance 8 captains at the First NC State Championship, and recieved the Judges' award.

Second Year: 2018 Power Up

The RobCoBots competed in the PowerUp competition season. In their first event, the NC District Pitt County Event, the RobCoBots placed 18th and made it to the Semi-Finals. In their second event, the NC District UNC Pembroke Event, the RobCoBots were Event winners and were awarded the Imagery award.

Third Year: 2019 Deep Space

The RobCoBots competed in the DeepSpace competition season. In their first event, the FNC District Guilford County Event, the RobCoBots were Event winners. In their second event, the FNC District UNC Pembroke Event, the RobCoBots placed 18th, made it to the quarter-finals, and were awarded the Judges' Award. To finish their third season the RobCoBots placed 25th at the First North Carolina State Championship, and were District Championship Finalists.

Fourth Year: 2020 Infinite Recharge

The RobCoBots competed in the cancelled Infinite Recharge competition season. Though this competition season was cut short due to the COVID-19 Pandemic, the RobCoBots competed at the FNC District UNC Pembroke Event. At this event, the RobCoBots placed 12th, made it to the quarter-finals, and were awarded the Engineering Inspiration award.

Sixth Year: 2022 Rapid React

After a competitive break due to the COVID-19 Pandemic. The RobCoBots returned to competition for the Rapid React competition season. At their first event, the FNC District ECU event, the RobCoBots placed 24th and won Innovation in Control award. To finish their compeition season, the RobCoBots were 6th Alliance captains, and made it to the quarter-finals.

Seventh Year: 2023 Charged Up

The RobCoBots competed in the Charged Up competition season. At their first event, the FNC District UNC Asheville Event, the RobCoBots placed 19th, won the judges award, and subbed in for Alliance 2 in the playoffs. To end their competition season, the RobCoBots were 4th Alliance captains at the FNC District UNC Pembroke Event, and won the Team Sustainability award.

Eigth Year: 2024 Crescendo

In their most recent competition season, Crescendo, the RobCoBots soared to new heights. To start their compeition season, the RobCobots placed 7th at the FNC District UNC Pembroke event, and were Finalists. At their second event, the FNC District Mecklenburg County Event, the RobCoBots placed 4th, and won the Team Spirit award. To end this compeition season, the RobCoBots placed third at the FIRST North Carolina District State Championship, and won the Judges' award.
