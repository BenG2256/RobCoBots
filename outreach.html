Outreach Events

Club Day

To start every year, the RobCoBots host their primary recruiting event, Club Day. Here the RobCoBots present their latest robot, as well as breifly present FIRST to students interested in joining the team.

Impact: 30-40 people

ETI Partnership Stem Event

In partnership with one of their primary sponsors, the RobCoBots hosted a Stem event for kids of all ages. At this event, the RobCoBots presented their robot and briefly discussed the importance of Stem and introduced FIRST to those in attendance.

Impact: 20-30 people

Annual Halloween Carnival

In order to help raise the necessary funds in order to fuel their outreach and competitive endeavors, the RobCoBots host an annual Halloween Carnival. This carnival is used to fundraise as well as bring their community together. Members of their community can enjoy carnival attractions such as games and even a maze.

Impact: 100 people

First Lego League Teams and Events

In partnership with their school system, the RobCoBots helped form 20 First Lego League teams. Additionally, the RobCoBots mentored these teams and presented the core values of FIRST. Furthermore, the RobCoBots hosted competitions for these FLL teams, so regardless of funding these teams could experience the complete FIRST experience including that of compeition.

Impact: 500+ people

First Tech Challenge Teams and Events

In an additional partnership with their school system, the RobCoBots helped form and mentor First Tech Challenge teams in every high school in their school district. The RobCoBots introduced their peers in other schools to FIRST and helped guide them on their journey with FIRST. Additionally, the RobCobots hosted and volunteered for local competitions for these FTC teams. In doing so, ensuring their local FTC teams can experience the complete FIRST experience, regardless of funding.

Impact: 1000+ people

Sponsors Meeting

As part of an annual tradition, the RobCoBots host a meeting and potluck with their generous sponsors. At this meeting, the RobCoBots display their latest robot, as well as their awards. In doing so, showing the competitive accomplishments supported by the sponsors in attendance. The RobCoBots additionally present their recent outreach endeavors, and the impact the sponsors support with their generous donations.

Impact: 50 people

Stem Event

Recently in partnership with Robeson Community College, the RobCoBots hosted a Stem event on their campus. At this event, attendees could enjoy fun games and science experiments. The RobCoBots also helped present and connect potential career outcomes in Stem to the fun games available at the Event. Additionally, the RobCoBots introduced FIRST and the opportunties available through FIRST to those in attedance.

Impact: 100 people

Steam Night

In partnership with their community, the RobCoBots volunteered at the local Steam night. At the event, those in attendance were introduced to Steam and its importance to our community. The RobCoBots introduced FIRST and its impact to those in attendance aswell.

Impact: 250 people

Parades

Each year the RobCoBots attend multiple local parades. At these parades, the RobCoBots display their robot, as well as FIRST to a large portion of their community.

Impact: 500 people

School Presentations

Each year the RobCoBots visit various local K-12 schools. While visiting these schools, the RobCoBots present Steam, FIRST, and their latest robot. Additionally, while presenting their robot the RobCoBots discuss the various intricacies and outside work that goes into making the robot. This exposes a large portion of their school system directly to FIRST as well as Steam.

Impact: 300 people
