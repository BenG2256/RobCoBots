Mission Statement

To increase stem awareness and knowledge within our ag-centric community and Title 1 school system.

Team Summary

Team 6729 The RobCoBots is a FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) Robotics team that was founded in the year of 2016 at Robeson Early College High School in Lumberton, North Carolina. We are a very diverse group that consists of 33 students and 10 mentors. Since we are from Robeson County, most of the student members come from low-income families. Our team consists of students of many ethnic backgrounds such as Asian, Hispanic, White, African American, and Native American.

Safety

The Safety Officer ensures that all safety precautions are taken in meetings, at competitions, and at community events. Each team member of The RobCoBots takes a safety quiz made by the Safety Officer before build season. Team members may use the safety book made by the Safety Officer as a study guide. The quiz is available for retake at any time. Once the quiz is passed, the team member will receive a safety badge to wear at competitions. Take a look at our Safety Manual
