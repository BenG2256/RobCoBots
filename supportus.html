Support The RobCoBots

Sponsor / Donation

As a part of a non-profit organization in FIRST, the registration and fee's associated with being an FRC team can be quite high. As such, the RobCoBots function largely off generous sponsors. Sponsors support the RobCoBots with donations of all kinds including money, tools, food, and even lodging. As the RobCoBots are a school based robotics team, all checks need to be made out to "PSRC Early College" with "Robotics Sponsorship" written on the memo line. Additionally, those interested in donating or sponsoring the RobCoBots, can view this pdf which briefly discusses the costs associated with being a robotics team.

Mentors

A FRC Robotics team is a lot like a small business. There are multiple aspects that go into a single robotics team such as: marketing, finance, public relations, electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, software, and strategy. Due to the sheer amount of work that goes into a robotics team, mentors are always welcomed.
